    Help & Support

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Everything you need to know about using SmartShare securely and efficiently.

    Is SmartShare really free? Do I need an account?
    Yes! SmartShare is 100% free. There are no accounts, no logins, no subscriptions, and no tracking. You can start sharing files instantly while remaining completely anonymous.
    What is the maximum file size I can send?
    Direct (P2P): Unlimited! Because Direct mode sends files straight from your device to the receiver without using our servers, there are no file size limits.

    Link Share (Cloud): Limited to 10MB per file (or 100MB for video files) to ensure our free cloud infrastructure remains fast for everyone.
    Are my files secure and encrypted?
    Absolutely. Direct Transfers use WebRTC with DTLS 1.3 encryption, creating a secure, end-to-end encrypted tunnel between the sender and receiver. We cannot see, intercept, or store your files.

    Link Shares are securely transmitted via HTTPS and stored in an isolated Firebase vault, which automatically permanently deletes your files once the timer expires or the download limit is reached.
    My Direct Transfer is stuck on "Connecting...". Why?
    This usually happens if one or both devices are on a strict corporate/university network, a VPN, or a "Symmetric NAT" firewall that blocks WebRTC P2P traffic.

    The Fix: Try switching one of the devices from Wi-Fi to Mobile Data (Cellular), or use the Link Share mode instead, which easily bypasses local network restrictions.
    Can I close the tab while sending a file?
    For Direct Transfers: No. Because the file is streaming directly from your device's memory, closing the tab will instantly kill the connection and cancel the transfer.

    For Link Shares: Yes! Once the progress bar reaches 100% and you see the "Share Code" screen, the file is safely in the cloud. You can close the tab and share the code whenever you want.
    How do I use the Native OS Share feature?
    To unlock native sharing, click the "Install App" button in the SmartShare menu (or select "Add to Home Screen" in your browser).

    Once installed as a Progressive Web App (PWA), SmartShare will appear in your phone's default share menu. You can open your Gallery or Files app, select a photo, tap "Share", and select SmartShare to instantly beam it to another device without ever opening your browser!
    What are "Trusted Devices" in Live Clipboard?
    If you frequently sync your clipboard between the same two devices (like your phone and your laptop), you can save them as "Trusted".

    Once saved, you'll see a button for that device in the Clipboard menu. Clicking it will instantly ping the other device and reconnect your clipboard—no need to scan a QR code or type a 6-digit sync ID ever again!
