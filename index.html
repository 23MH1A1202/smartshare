    We are a free, independent hybrid file-sharing service. Our mission is to keep your data in your hands—exactly as it should be.

    Straight from your device

    When using Direct sharing, we store absolutely nothing online. Your files never touch a server—close the tab and you stop sharing instantly.

    Auto-resume & smart recovery

    SmartShare keeps transfers stable on shaky networks. If your connection drops, the transfer pauses and resumes once you are back.

    No limits in Direct mode

    Because we do not store your data during Direct sharing, you can send files of any size. You are only limited by your connection.

    Only the receiver can access

    Only you and the receiver can access your files. Your data is encrypted end-to-end, and can only be read by your receiver. SmartShare currently uses an implementation of DTLS 1.3.

    Self-cleaning Link shares

    Link shares expire automatically on your schedule. Set a timer or a download limit and let SmartShare clean up for you.

    Real-Time Clipboard & Trusted Devices

    Instantly sync text, links, and images. Save frequent hardware to your Trusted Devices list for instant, one-tap reconnects! Our rich-text pad supports drag-and-drop and a continuous background heartbeat to prevent drops.

    Built-in HD QR Scanner

    No need for third-party scanning apps. SmartShare features a blazing-fast, custom-built native QR scanner right in your browser. Just point your camera to pair devices instantly, with auto-contrast correction for glowing screens.

    Native OS Share Integration

    Install SmartShare as a PWA and it seamlessly integrates into your device's native share menu. Select files directly from your Android Gallery or Files app, tap "Share", and send them instantly without opening the browser!

    Direct, Link & Clipboard — In Detail

    Each mode is designed for a specific moment: instant device-to-device sharing, secure cloud links, or live clipboard sync.

    Direct (Device-to-Device)

    Direct creates an encrypted WebRTC tunnel between two devices, so your files never touch a server.

    • Share using a code, built-in QR scan, or link for fast pairing.
    • Auto-pause and resume transfers if your connection drops.
    • End the session instantly by closing the tab.

    Link Share (Cloud Vault)

    Link Share stores encrypted files in the cloud with a short code, timed expiry, and download limits.

    • Files upload to Cloud.
    • Set expirations, one-time downloads, or custom codes.
    • Manage, extend, or delete links from the Active Links screen.

    Live Clipboard

    Live Clipboard keeps text, links, and images synced in real time between your devices.

    • Trusted Devices: Save frequent hardware for instant 1-tap connections without re-entering codes.
    • Rich-text pad supports drag-and-drop images and smart URL detection.
    • Background heartbeat keeps sessions alive and reports disconnects.

    Help & Support

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Everything you need to know about using SmartShare securely and efficiently.

    Is SmartShare really free? Do I need an account?
    Yes! SmartShare is 100% free. There are no accounts, no logins, no subscriptions, and no tracking. You can start sharing files instantly while remaining completely anonymous.
    What is the maximum file size I can send?
    Direct (P2P): Unlimited! Because Direct mode sends files straight from your device to the receiver without using our servers, there are no file size limits.

    Link Share (Cloud): Limited to 10MB per file (or 100MB for video files) to ensure our free cloud infrastructure remains fast for everyone.
    Are my files secure and encrypted?
    Absolutely. Direct Transfers use WebRTC with DTLS 1.3 encryption, creating a secure, end-to-end encrypted tunnel between the sender and receiver. We cannot see, intercept, or store your files.

    Link Shares are securely transmitted via HTTPS and stored in an isolated Firebase vault, which automatically permanently deletes your files once the timer expires or the download limit is reached.
    My Direct Transfer is stuck on "Connecting...". Why?
    This usually happens if one or both devices are on a strict corporate/university network, a VPN, or a "Symmetric NAT" firewall that blocks WebRTC P2P traffic.

    The Fix: Try switching one of the devices from Wi-Fi to Mobile Data (Cellular), or use the Link Share mode instead, which easily bypasses local network restrictions.
    Can I close the tab while sending a file?
    For Direct Transfers: No. Because the file is streaming directly from your device's memory, closing the tab will instantly kill the connection and cancel the transfer.

    For Link Shares: Yes! Once the progress bar reaches 100% and you see the "Share Code" screen, the file is safely in the cloud. You can close the tab and share the code whenever you want.
    How do I use the Native OS Share feature?
    To unlock native sharing, click the "Install App" button in the SmartShare menu (or select "Add to Home Screen" in your browser).

    Once installed as a Progressive Web App (PWA), SmartShare will appear in your phone's default share menu. You can open your Gallery or Files app, select a photo, tap "Share", and select SmartShare to instantly beam it to another device without ever opening your browser!
    What are "Trusted Devices" in Live Clipboard?
    If you frequently sync your clipboard between the same two devices (like your phone and your laptop), you can save them as "Trusted".

    Once saved, you'll see a button for that device in the Clipboard menu. Clicking it will instantly ping the other device and reconnect your clipboard—no need to scan a QR code or type a 6-digit sync ID ever again!
