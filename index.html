    We are a free, independent hybrid file-sharing service. Our mission is to keep your data in your hands—exactly as it should be.

    Straight from your device

    When using Direct sharing, we store absolutely nothing online. Your files never touch a server—close the tab and you stop sharing instantly.

    Auto-resume & smart recovery

    SmartShare keeps transfers stable on shaky networks. If your connection drops, the transfer pauses and resumes once you are back.

    No limits in Direct mode

    Because we do not store your data during Direct sharing, you can send files of any size. You are only limited by your connection.

    Only the receiver can access your files

    Only you and the receiver can access your files. Your data is encrypted end-to-end, and can only be read by your receiver (and you of course). SmartShare currently uses an implementation of DTLS 1.3.

    Self-cleaning Link shares

    Link shares expire automatically on your schedule. Set a timer or a download limit and let SmartShare clean up for you.

    Real-Time Live Clipboard

    Instantly sync text, clickable links, and images across devices. Our rich-text pad supports drag-and-drop, smart URL detection, and a continuous background heartbeat so your session never drops. Disconnections are caught and reported instantly.

    Low environmental impact

    By bypassing bulky, energy-hungry server farms and routing data directly between phones and laptops, you have a much smaller carbon footprint than when using traditional big-tech cloud storage providers.
